## 💡 Pro Tips

### For AI Discovery
- AI models prioritize **authoritative, factual content**
- Use **clear hierarchies** (H1 → H2 → H3)
- Answer questions **directly** (AI loves concise answers)
- Include **data and numbers** (specs, measurements, prices)
- Create **comparison content** (AI uses this for recommendations)

### For Google
- **E-E-A-T** matters: Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trust
- **User intent** is everything: Match content to search intent
- **Core Web Vitals** affect rankings
- **Backlinks** from outdoor/gear sites > generic links
- **Fresh content** signals > stale 4-year-old posts
