# NakedSlope — Brand Voice Guide

**Tagline:** No fluff. Just gear.

---

## The Voice in One Sentence

Write like a knowledgeable friend who actually uses the gear — not a gear journalist trying to avoid liability.

---

## Tone Principles

**Unfiltered**
Say what's bad about a product. If the $400 version isn't worth it, say so.
Bad: "Some users may find the retention system takes getting used to."
Good: "The BOA dial feels cheap for a $280 helmet. It works, but it'll annoy you."

**Direct**
Lead with the answer. No preamble.
Bad: "In today's market, there are many ski helmets to choose from..."
Good: "The Smith Vantage MIPS is the best ski helmet for most people."

**Specific**
Numbers over adjectives.
Bad: "This wetsuit is very warm."
Good: "This wetsuit handles water temps down to 50°F without needing a hood."

**Earned credibility**
Don't fake expertise. Attribute where needed. "According to Patagonia's lab testing..." is fine.

---

## What We Don't Do

- Affiliate disclaimers buried in the footer (put them at the top)
- "In conclusion..." or "Final thoughts..."
- Passive voice in product sections
- Vague hedging: "may", "could potentially", "in some cases"
- Recommending products we haven't researched deeply

---

## Post Openers — Templates

**Commercial roundup:**
> The [PRODUCT CATEGORY] market is full of overpriced garbage and a few actually good options. Here's what we'd buy.

**Informational guide:**
> [PROBLEM SCENARIO]. Here's what you actually need to know.

**Comparison post:**
> [Product A] vs [Product B] comes down to one thing: [key differentiator]. Here's how to know which one you need.

---

## CTAs

Keep them direct:
- "Check price at Evo →"
- "Buy on Amazon →"
- "See current price →"
- NOT: "Click here to learn more about this amazing product!"

---

## Article Length Guidelines

| Type                | Min words | Max words | Note                          |
|---------------------|-----------|-----------|-------------------------------|
| Commercial roundup  | 2,500     | 4,000     | Don't pad — every word earns  |
| Informational guide | 1,500     | 2,800     | Cut anything not actionable   |
| Comparison (2-3 products) | 1,200 | 2,000  | Tight, decisive               |
| Category hub page   | 600       | 1,000     | Curate, don't repeat content  |
